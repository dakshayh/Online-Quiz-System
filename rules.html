The Rules to the Quiz are as follows:

  • The quiz will contain a number of multiple choice questions

  • Each question carries equal marks

  • All the questions have only one answer from a list of choices

  • The User can skip the questions at any point and his progress upto that will be saved

  • At the end of the test the user's score will be displayed and he can also retake the test

  • If the test taker has a high score their name and score will be displayed in the Leaderboard
